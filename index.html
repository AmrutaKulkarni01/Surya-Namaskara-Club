What is Suryanamaskara

  • Surya Namaskar is the Sanskrit name for ancient yogic practice
  • Consists of a specific sequence of twelve yoga asana
  • one of the most important yoga practices
  • Known as Sun Salutation in English

Benefits of Suryanamaskara

  • Provides a complete work-out for body, mind and spirit
  • Stimulates all muscles, organs, systems and chakras
  • Cultivate concentration and stillness of mind
  • Energizing and efficient way to connect with inner strength and stability
